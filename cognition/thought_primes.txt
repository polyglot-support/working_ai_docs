Sensory Primes:

Visual: Basic colors (red, blue, yellow), brightness, darkness

Tactile: Pressure, temperature, texture
Olfactory: Basic smell categories (floral, putrid, etc.)
Gustatory: Basic tastes (sweet, sour, salty, bitter, umami)

Direction: up, down, left, right, forward, backward
Time: now, before, after, duration
Size: big, small, growing, shrinking
Distance: near, far, closer, farther

Basic emotions: joy, sadness, anger, fear, disgust, surprise
Intensity levels of emotions
Emotional valence: positive, negative, neutral

Existence: being, not being
Quantity: one, many, more, less

Similarity: same, different
Causality: cause, effect

State changes: start, stop, continue, change

Possession: have, not have
Location: in, out, on, under
Communication Primes:

Intention: inform, request, warn
Agreement: yes, no, maybe
Emphasis: important, unimportant
Physiological State Primes:

Energy levels: tired, energetic
Comfort: pain, pleasure, neutral
Cognitive Process Primes:

Attention: focus, ignore
Memory: remember, forget
Decision: choose, avoid
Meta-Cognitive Primes:

Certainty: know, unsure

Preference: like, dislike

Coordination: balance, stumble

Biological Primes:

Life processes: breathe, eat, sleep, grow, decay
